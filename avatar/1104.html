Gepard

A captain in the Silvermane Guards and an outstanding warrior of Belobog.
He is meticulous and vigilant to the core and is always true to himself.

ID: 1104
Path: Preservation
Element: Ice
Rarity: 5*

Stats

  • Max HP: 1397.09
  • Max ATK: 543.31
  • Max DEF: 654.88
  • Speed: 92
  • Taunt: 150
  • CRIT Rate: 0.05
  • CRIT DMG: 0.5

Skills

  • Fist of Conviction (Basic ATK):
    Deals Ice DMG equal to #1[i] of Gepard's ATK to a single enemy.
  • Daunting Smite (Skill):
    Deals Ice DMG equal to #1[i] of Gepard's ATK to a single enemy, with a #2[i] base chance to Freeze the enemy for #3[i] turn(s).
    While Frozen, the enemy cannot take action and will take Additional Ice DMG equal to #4[i] of Gepard's ATK at the beginning of each turn.
  • Enduring Bulwark (Ultimate):
    Applies a Shield to all allies, absorbing DMG equal to #1[i] of Gepard's DEF plus #3[i] for #2[i] turn(s).
  • Unyielding Will (Talent):
    When struck with a killing blow, instead of becoming knocked down, Gepard's HP immediately restores to #1[i] of his Max HP. This effect can only trigger once per battle.
  • Attack (Unknown):
    Attacks an enemy, and when the battle starts, reduces their Toughness of the corresponding Type.
  • Comradery (Technique):
    After Gepard uses his Technique, when the next battle begins, a Shield will be applied to all allies, absorbing DMG equal to #1[i] of Gepard's DEF plus #3[i] for #2[i] turn(s).

Ascension Traces

  • Integrity:
    Gepard has a higher chance to be attacked by enemies.
  • Commander:
    When "Unyielding Will" is triggered, Gepard's Energy will be restored to 100%.
  • Grit:
    Gepard's ATK increases by 35% of his current DEF. This effect will refresh at the start of each turn.

Eidolons

  • Due Diligence:
    When using Skill, increases the base chance to Freeze target enemy by 35%.
  • Lingering Cold:
    After an enemy Frozen by Skill is unfrozen, their SPD is reduced by 20% for 1 turn(s).
  • Never Surrender:
    Ultimate Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.
    Talent Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.
  • Faith Moves Mountains:
    When Gepard is in battle, all allies' Effect RES increases by 20%.
  • Cold Iron Fist:
    Skill Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.
    Basic ATK Lv. +1, up to a maximum of Lv. 10.
  • Unyielding Resolve:
    When his Talent is triggered, Gepard immediately takes action again and restores extra HP equal to 50% of his Max HP.
