Pela

An intelligence officer for the Silvermane Guards.
She has a serious personality and is revered by other members of the Silvermane Guards.

ID: 1106
Path: Nihility
Element: Ice
Rarity: 4*

Skills

  • Frost Shot (Basic ATK):
    Deals Ice DMG equal to #1[i] of Pela's ATK to a single target enemy.
  • Frostbite (Skill):
    Removes #2[i] buff(s) and deals Ice DMG equal to #1[i] of Pela's ATK to a single target enemy.
  • Zone Suppression (Ultimate):
    Deals Ice DMG equal to #4[i] of Pela's ATK to all enemies, with a #1[i] base chance to inflict Exposed on all enemies.
    When Exposed, enemies' DEF is reduced by #2[i] for #3[i] turn(s).
  • Data Collecting (Talent):
    If the enemy is debuffed after Pela's attack, Pela will restore #1[f1] additional Energy. This effect can only be triggered 1 time per attack.
  • Attack (Unknown):
    Attacks an enemy, and when the battle starts, reduces their Toughness of the corresponding Type.
  • Preemptive Strike (Technique):
    Immediately attacks the enemy. Upon entering battle, Pela deals Ice DMG equal to #4[i] of her ATK to a random enemy, with a #1[i] base chance of lowering the DEF of all enemies by #2[i] for #3[i] turn(s).
