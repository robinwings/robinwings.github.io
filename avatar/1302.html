Argenti

A paragon knight of the Knights of Beauty who is piously seeking his missing Aeon, Idrila the Beauty.
Forthright and candid, he wanders the cosmos espousing the virtues of Idrila's good name.

ID: 1302
Path: Erudition
Element: Physical
Rarity: 5*

Stats

  • Max HP: 1047.82
  • Max ATK: 737.35
  • Max DEF: 363.83
  • Speed: 103
  • Taunt: 75
  • CRIT Rate: 0.05
  • CRIT DMG: 0.5

Skills

  • Fleeting Fragrance (Basic ATK):
    Deals Physical DMG equal to #1[i] of Argenti's ATK to a single target enemy.
  • Justice, Hereby Blooms (Skill):
    Deals Physical DMG equal to #1[i] of Argenti's ATK to all enemies.
  • For In This Garden, Supreme Beauty Bestows (Ultimate):
    Consumes #2[i] Energy and deals Physical DMG equal to #1[i] of Argenti's ATK to all enemies.
  • Sublime Object (Talent):
    For every enemy hit when Argenti uses his Basic Attack, Skill, or Ultimate, regenerates Argenti's Energy by #1[i], and grants him a stack of Apotheosis, increasing his CRIT Rate by #2[f1]. This effect can stack up to #3[i] time(s).
  • Attack (Unknown):
    Attacks an enemy, and when the battle starts, reduces their Toughness of the corresponding Type.
  • Manifesto of Purest Virtue (Technique):
    After using the Technique, enemies in a set area are inflicted with Daze for #1[i] second(s). Dazed enemies will not actively attack the team.
    When attacking a Dazed enemy to enter combat, deals Physical DMG to all enemies equal to #2[i] of Argenti's ATK and regenerates his Energy by #3[i].
  • Merit Bestowed in "My" Garden (Ultimate):
    Consumes #4[i] Energy and deals Physical DMG equal to #1[i] of Argenti's ATK to all enemies. And further deals DMG for #2[i] extra time(s), with each time dealing Physical DMG equal to #3[i] of Argenti's ATK to a random enemy.
